Research internship

We are actively looking for research interns to join our lab all year around. If you are looking for an exciting research on robot intelligence, please fill out this form

What role would I play? You will be contributing to a real research project that expands the boundary of human knowledge. You will be ideating and creating new technologies that will advance the robot intelligence to the next level. With some of the most talented team members, You will learn what it means to work with great efficiency and effort on challenging problems that no one has answers to. You will NOT be a code monkey for some government or company grants, or be a clerk for administrative work.

Where is the lab? We are located in KAIST Seoul campus.

How much is the compensation? We pay monthly salary of 400,000won. For those of you who get a housing near KAIST seoul campus, we will cover your rent in up to 400,000won per month.

Notes on diversity Considering the weight and difficulty of the problems we are tackling at our lab, we deeply understand the need to attract talents all around the globe regardless of their sex, race, and age. We are striving for an embracing culture, so please do not hesitate to apply.
